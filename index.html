MRR Finder Bot

Quick Guide & Reference

Commands

/rental Find rigs by PH target
/rentalth Find rigs by TH target
/best Find best value rigs for time & budget

Parameters

balance

Your available balance

Example: 0.005
target_ph / target_th

Desired hashrate

Example: 1 for 1 PH or 500 for 500 TH
hours

Target rental duration

Example: 12 for 12 hours
algorithm

Payment currency:

BTC - Pay with Bitcoin (default) ETH - Pay with Ethereum

Examples

/rental balance:0.005 target_ph:1 algorithm:ETH
/rentalth balance:0.038 target_th:500 algorithm:ETH
/best balance:0.001 hours:12 algorithm:ETH
